Frequently Asked Questions

How does Ceres Cart work?

Ceres Cart is a Chrome extension that enhances your recipe browsing experience. It automatically pops up when you view a recipe, helping you shop for ingredients with ease. Whether you're looking for budget-friendly meals or accommodating dietary needs like vegan or gluten-free options, Ceres Cart simplifies the process. Eat healthier by making shopping simpler!

Can Ceres Cart bypass paywalls on recipe sites?

Yes, Ceres Cart can bypass paywalls on select recipe websites, including:

  • The New York Times
  • America’s Test Kitchen
  • Epicurious

If you encounter a site where it doesn’t work, let us know, and we’ll strive to support it.

Who is Ceres?

Ceres is the Roman goddess of agriculture, symbolizing harvest, growth, and nourishment. She is the inspiration behind Ceres Cart, reflecting our mission to make cooking and eating simpler and more accessible.

What inspired the creation of Ceres Cart?

Ceres Cart was born out of frustration with recipe websites overloaded with ads and distractions. Our goal is to provide a streamlined experience so you can focus on cooking delicious meals without interruptions.

How can I link my grocery cart with Ceres Cart?

Ceres Cart automatically prompts you to link your grocery cart when you start shopping for ingredients. Follow the on-screen instructions to sync with your preferred store.

How do I uninstall a Chrome extension?

If you’re unhappy with Ceres Cart or no longer need it:

  1. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions/.
  2. Locate Ceres Cart in your extensions list.
  3. Click "Remove" and confirm.

We’re sad to see you go! Please share feedback so we can improve: Feedback Form

What websites does Ceres Cart work for?

Ceres Cart works on most popular recipe websites. If you discover one where it doesn’t work, please contact us, and we’ll work to add support for it.

How does Ceres Cart make money?

Ceres Cart earns revenue through affiliate links. When you shop for ingredients via the extension, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. This allows us to keep the extension free for users.

What are the benefits of using Ceres Cart?

Using Ceres Cart offers numerous benefits:

  • Skip long personal stories and ads on recipe websites.
  • Enjoy a clean, universal interface for all recipe sites.
  • Save money by purchasing only what you need.
  • Reduce food waste by knowing exactly how much to buy.
  • Plan meals efficiently to match your dietary preferences and budget.
