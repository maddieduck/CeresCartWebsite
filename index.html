Ceres Cart is a chrome extension that offers a streamlined method for transferring ingredients from your favorite recipes to your online grocery cart.

Auto-Detects Recipes
Automatically shows up when a user views a recipe
Shop Nearby
Input a zip code. Once a store is selected, accurate pricing for items becomes available.
Select Items
Press arrows to pan through ingredients. Add ingredients with the +.
Add Items
Adding items adds items directly to your cart. Go to the store's website to finish checkout.

Integrations

Presently, our app exclusively integrates with Kroger, ensuring seamless grocery shopping experiences. Rest assured, we're diligently expanding our partnerships to include more grocery stores in the near future.

Pricing Options

Our extension offers flexible pricing options to suit every user's needs. With our free tier, users can enjoy the app's features at no cost, including the ability to export up to three recipes. For those who require more, we offer a premium subscription at just $0.99 a month, unlocking unlimited recipe exports. With transparent and straightforward pricing, our goal is to provide accessible and valuable services, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the benefits of our app.
