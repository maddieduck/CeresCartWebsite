Ceres Cart offers a streamlined method for transferring ingredients from your favorite recipes to your online grocery cart.

What We Offer

Ceres Cart seamlessly enhances your cooking experience, by automatically identifying recipes on your webpage and allows users to shop for groceries from nearby Kroger and Kroger affiliate stores without the need for embedded checkout options. By extracting ingredients directly from the page, it overcomes limitations even when website integrations are absent.

Automatic Recipe Detection

Extension automatically appears on your webpage when you are viewing a recipe.

Location Selection

Select a location closest to you and Ceres Cart will remember it for next time. View the most current price for a product.

Select Items

Add the items you desire. See the total price in the checkout button.

Add to Cart

Press the checkout button to add the items to your online grocery cart.

Integrations

Presently, our app exclusively integrates with Kroger, ensuring seamless grocery shopping experiences. Rest assured, we're diligently expanding our partnerships to include more grocery stores in the near future.

Pricing Options

Our app offers flexible pricing options to suit every user's needs. With our free tier, users can enjoy the app's features at no cost, including the ability to export up to three recipes. For those who require more, we offer a premium subscription at just $0.99 a month, unlocking unlimited recipe exports. With transparent and straightforward pricing, our goal is to provide accessible and valuable services, ensuring that everyone can enjoy the benefits of our app.
