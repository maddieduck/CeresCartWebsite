Ceres Cart is a chrome extension that offers a streamlined method for transferring ingredients from your favorite recipes to your online grocery cart.

Auto-Detects Recipes
Automatically shows up when a user views a recipe on their Desktop
Shop Nearby
Input a zip code. Once a store is selected, accurate pricing for items becomes available.
Select Items
Press arrows to pan through ingredients. Add ingredients with the +.
Add Items
Adding items adds items directly to your online cart.

Integrations

Presently, our AI-powered app exclusively integrates with Kroger and Walmart, ensuring seamless grocery shopping experiences. Rest assured, we're diligently expanding our partnerships to include more grocery stores in the near future.

Pricing Options
