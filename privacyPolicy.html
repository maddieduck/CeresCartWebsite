Ceres Cart Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ceres Cart. This privacy policy describes how we collect and use the personal information you provide when using our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on the service you wish to use, you will be asked to log in to your grocery store account, from which we will share that information with the grocery store to access your account.

3. Use of Information

We do not share any personal information with anyone outside of grocery store vendors.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at contact@cerescart.com.
